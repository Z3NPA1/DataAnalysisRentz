A modern space full of calming bedroom ideas

Welcome to a small, modern-style bedroom with clean, simple lines and beautiful empty spaces. Sleek, organised storage makes sure there’s no clutter and helps create a calm place to recharge. And you can set the mood in an instant as much of the lighting and more are smart products controlled from the IKEA Home smart app on your phone.

A few simple touches

A few carefully chosen and well co-ordinated pieces can create a big effect. The secret of this look is keeping things simple by having a base in mostly natural tones and adding some interesting textures.

    A holiday every night

    With its fresh, crisp bed linen, sense of space and lightness, and electric blinds that you can pull up or down while you’re still in bed, this bedroom feels like a luxury hotel without having to leave home. The colours, from rug to curtains, are in harmony, helping it all look calm.
